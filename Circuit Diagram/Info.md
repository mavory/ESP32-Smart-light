Documentation for the Smart light Control System!!

## Wiring

### LED Strip (MOSFET)
- Gate: GPIO 27
- VCC: 5V (External)
- GND: GND

### NeoPixel Ring (24 LEDs)
- Data In: GPIO 13
- VCC: 5V
- GND: GND

### Sound Sensor (Microphone)
- Digital Out: GPIO 34
- VCC: 3.3V/5V
- GND: GND

### Main Button
- Pin: GPIO 26
- GND: GND

### Buzzer
- Pin: GPIO 25
- GND: GND

### RGB Status LED (Common Cathode)
- Red: GPIO 18
- Green: GPIO 19
- Blue: GPIO 21
- Cathode: GND

---

## Technical Notes
- **Power:** The LED strip requires an external 5V power supply.
